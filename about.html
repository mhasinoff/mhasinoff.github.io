What and Who is Mountain Time Mapping?

I'm Michael Hasinoff and right now I am the only one behind Mountain Time Mapping.

I can't wait until I can take my daughters backpacking to Berg Lake which lays in the shadow of Mt. Robson, the highest mountain in Canada. The topo map above is for the provincial park in which Mt. Robson resides.
